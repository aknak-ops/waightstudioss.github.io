Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

Waight Studios (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our apps, including NextDay, Athia Fit, and any other apps we develop and publish.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or use certain features, we may collect:

  • Your email address (for authentication and communication)
  • Anonymous usage data (to improve app performance)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics (via Firebase Crashlytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email is used to:

  • Log you into the app using Firebase Authentication
  • Identify your saved data (e.g., progress, plans, preferences)
  • Send important app-related updates (we do not send marketing emails without consent)

3. Data Storage & Security

All user data is securely stored using trusted services such as Firebase. We take appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Account Deletion

You may request to delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at waightstudioss@gmail.com. Upon request, we will permanently erase your account and data within a reasonable timeframe.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services:

  • Firebase Authentication – for email-based sign-in
  • Firebase Firestore/Realtime Database – to store user preferences and progress
  • Firebase Crashlytics – to detect and fix bugs
  • Google Play Services – for updates and compatibility

6. Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page regularly for updates.
